Multiply each time/frequency matrix of this fft_array \( A \) from the left by the hermitian conjugate of a complex matrix \( M \): \( A(t) \to M^\dagger \cdot A(t) \).

Parameters
MThe matrix to left-multiply with (must be size1_ × size1_)
